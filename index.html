Meet The Doctors

When it comes to your child’s oral health, you want to be sure they’re well looked after. For a dental partner who understands their unique needs and can ensure their comfort, you can put your trust in Ari Pediatric Dentistry. As Board-certified pediatric dentists in London, we know how to help the most anxious patients relax, including kids with special needs. We take a holistic approach to care, and because we do, we encourage patients to lead a healthy lifestyle.
